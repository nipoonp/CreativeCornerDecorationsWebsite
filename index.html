Our Story

There's something truly magical about the collection of details that pulls an event together and to be trusted with that special occasion is incredibly gratifying. Creative Corner Decorations came from a need to create and was built on absolute passion and love, with every day that passion only grows. I can honestly say that there is nowhere else I'd rather be. I still immerse myself in every event as though it's the first, I still stand back in awe with what we have created and I still feel that heart bursting pride when you call and tell me that it couldn't have been more perfect.

We are a small boutique team and our service reflects that. We recognise that each event is unique and take pride in providing a service that is completely personalised. We don't do "cookie cutter" here and our aim is always to exceed expectations, we can proudly say that we have accomplished that so far.

I hope you enjoy looking through the work my team and I have created and I would love to hear from you to personally discuss your special event.

Services

Weddings

  • To bust the first myth, a wedding planner does NOT need to be expensive - you can get our support for as little as $600!
  • We can tailor make a package to suit your needs and budget by helping just a little, or a lot
  • The Queen package is the full wedding planning suite, where pink apple covers every detail of the preparation, planning and execution leaving you with a completely stress free wedding - pure bliss.
  • The Dame package is our most cost effective and with this package we come in a month before the wedding to manage and coordinate all your suppliers/vendors. We confirm all your vendors are delivering according to their contracts, remove the pre-wedding stress and make sure everything has been checked (and rechecked) and nothing has been missed. We will be present on the day to execute your wedding
  • Our Princess and Duchess packages offer varying levels of our involvement between the two above
  • As well as organising the wedding, we hire out awesome wedding decor - and can deliver, set-up and pack-down too.
  • As every wedding is different, get in touch today for a complimentary meeting to discuss your specific requirements and how we can add sparkle to your wedding

Events & parties

  • Creative Cornor Decorations is an affordable planner for any type of party or event - we love them all.
  • We can arrange parties Auckland wide, but can also travel as far as you need
  • We will source and select all the suppliers/vendors required, arrange decor, invites, set-up etc
  • You can have as much or as little support as you need - we will make sure you relax and actually enjoy your party :-)
  • We have extensive experience in planning baby showers, christenings, kids and adult birthday parties, engagements, corporate functions etc

Cost

The wedding and event planning costs are determined based on your specific requirements so it's best to get in touch and arrange a no obligations discussion

As an estimate for wedding planning, the cost can range between $600 - $1400 for wedding day coordination only and between $3000 - $5000 for the complete wedding planning package (Queen package).

The cool thing about choosing us

We LOVE planning parties and weddings!!!!

For the last 20 years I have been the trusted party planner for friends and family - and I have loved every event. I have arranged baby showers, christenings, hen parties, birthdays, holidays etc! The more I planned, the more I realised how much I loved the job and wanted to make it my career.

I was married in Auckland in January 2017 and planned the wedding myself while living in the UK and holding down a demanding job. Following our wedding, I was addicted to the country as well as the role of wedding planning. So hence the career path started and I decided to make my dream job a reality....
